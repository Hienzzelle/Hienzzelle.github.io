Who is Hienzzelle
Pantoja?
A Third-Year Digital Artist student taking Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Cavite State University-CCAT in the Philippines.

Maria Anna Hienzzelle T. Pantoja is a 22-year-old student born on March 9, 2003 currently living in Tanza, Cavite. This showcases her journey as a student passionate about arts & crafts and programming. She is continuously learning and developing skills in programming, web designing and digital arts.

From a young age, she has always dreamed of becoming an artist, and her passion for creativity has driven her to pursue this dream with dedication. Whether through drawing, painting, or other artistic endeavors, she finds joy in expressing herself through her art. While she enjoys coding and the problem-solving aspects of programming, her heart still lies in the arts. She hopes to combine both worlds in the future, perhaps creating innovative digital art or designing for the tech industry.
